Electrohydrodynamics

After doing an extensive literature review and consulting some pioneers in the field I started to work on this project where I will be aiming to solve the famous Poisson-Boltzmann equation coupled with Navier-Stokes in a two dimensional channel. The twist I have incorporated in this is by omitting the age-old Debye-Huckle approximation(to separate non-linearity) and my favourite boundary element formulation to tackle non-linear equations.
