Welcome to my website

Hi, I am passionate about solving real life theoretical and experimental fluid dynamics problems using advanced numerical schemes to overcome the heavy computational cost associated with the high-fidelity simulations. Fluid dynamics plays a pivotal role in various engineering and scientific applications, ranging from aerodynamics in aerospace engineering to hydrodynamics in marine engineering, and even in biomedical applications such as blood flow simulation. I have been working on meshless computational formulations (like boundary element method, smooth particle hydrodynamics, meshless local petrov galerkin method, etc.) that would reduce the time complexity of the given problem. The Boundary Element Method offers a promising approach to model and analyze fluid behavior by discretizing the boundary of the domain of interest, which often leads to more efficient computations by reducing the dimensionality of the problem in comparison to other traditional methods like Finite Element or Finite Volume methods. , but you can call me telecomsteve.
